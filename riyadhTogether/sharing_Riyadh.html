Applying the analysis in Riyadh

While this project used data from New York City's transit system and taxis to show how shared mobility can impact first- and last-mile trips to subway stations, it is critical for policy makers to be proactive in developing a shared mobility plan to serve the future Metro system.

The analysis presented in this project used historic data to model potential shared trip scenarios, but the methodology could be adapted to develop a trip matching app for matching potential shared trips to or from stations within the Riyadh Metro system.
